Cheng Feng

Bio

I am currently a senior research scientist at Siemens Corporate Technology (西门子研究院), working on industrial data analytics and artificial intelligence research.

Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute for Security Science and Technology, Imperial College London.

I obtained my PhD in Quantitative Modelling with Professor Jane Hillston at the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science , School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh.

My research interests are in the areas of performance modelling and machine learning. Over the past years, I have been creating and applying formal modelling and machine learning techniques to help build more intelligent, secure and dependable systems for smart city and smart manufacturing applications.

I serve as a journal reviewer for:
  • IEEE Transactions on Reliability
  • IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems
  • IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing
  • ACM Transactions on Internet of Things
