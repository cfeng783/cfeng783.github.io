Cheng Feng

Bio

I am a senior key expert research scientist at Siemens Technology (西门子研究院,北京), working in the area of industrial data analytics and artificial intelligence.

During 2016/09 to 2018/02, I was a Research Associate in AI and System Security at the Institute for Security Science and Technology, Imperial College London.

In 2016, I obtained my PhD in Quantitative Modelling with Professor Jane Hillston at the Laboratory for Foundations of Computer Science , School of Informatics, University of Edinburgh.

My research focuses on probabilistic modelling and inference for industrial dynamical systems, with applications including time series forecasting, anomaly detection, control optimization and performance evaluation.

I am looking for highly motivated interns to work with me on above areas. Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested.
